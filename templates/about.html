About OptiCrop

Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine

OptiCrop is a Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine developed using Machine Learning.

This application predicts the most suitable crop based on Nitrogen, Phosphorous, Potassium, Temperature, Humidity, pH and Rainfall.

  • 🌱 Nitrogen (N)
  • 🌾 Phosphorous (P)
  • 🍃 Potassium (K)
  • 🌡 Temperature
  • 💧 Humidity
  • ⚗ pH Value
  • 🌧 Rainfall

The objective of this project is to improve agricultural productivity and help farmers select the most suitable crop using Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Find Your Crop Page

Clicking Find Your Crop redirects to the prediction page. Enter the environmental conditions and click Predict to receive the recommended crop.
